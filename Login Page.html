West Bengal, located in eastern India, is known for its rich culture, vibrant festivals, and diverse landscapes. Kolkata, the capital, boasts colonial architecture and artistic heritage. The Sundarbans, home to the Royal Bengal Tiger, offers breathtaking natural beauty. Renowned for its literature, music, and cuisine, West Bengal is a cultural gem.
